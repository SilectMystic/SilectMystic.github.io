Robotics

Robotics is one of the extra activities that I like to do a lot in my free time.



Being an active member of FIRST Robotics Competition Team 354 G-House Pirates during the competition in 2023 was our team's true comeback by having a decently sized team and also two of our alumni coming back as mentors we were able to build a far better robot which turned out to be a stronger and super fast robot compared to 2022 which we only had 2 students and a smaller room.

For 2024 we are working on competing for 2 one on NYC Regional which is a 6-week competition and at Hudson Valley which is a 2 week competition and also getting our students more practice with tools and getting them more experience in both designing and building.
